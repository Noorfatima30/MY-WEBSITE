LAHORE

Lahore is the capital city of the Punjab province in Pakistan and is the second-largest city in the country. It is known as the cultural, educational, and artistic hub of Pakistan, with a rich history that dates back to ancient times. Lahore is famous for its Mughal-era architecture, including the iconic Badshahi Mosque and Lahore Fort.

Paris

Paris has been one of the world's major centres of finance, diplomacy, commerce, fashion, gastronomy, and science. Paris is home to a whopping 130 museums

London

London, the capital of England and the United Kingdom, is a 21st-century city with history stretching back to Roman times.
